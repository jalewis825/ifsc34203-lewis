The Cool Cat

Our highly curated cat groom will include anything your cat will allow us to perform. We will lead the groom with being as stress free and safe as possible. If allowed, we will thoroughly brush, de-shed, bathe, dry, trim nails, and cut your cat's fur to your specification.

Cat grooms are highly specialized and require an extra amount of detail to ensure everyone remains as safe and happy as possible. Due to this, prices will vary depending on your cat's coat and demeanor. All cat grooms start at $65 for a bath, dry, and nail trim and will increase based on services performed.

The Lowdown on Us

Our Story is the real McCoy!

Meet Mac

Mac Daddy Grooming was a labor of love brought forth from the desire to build a safe grooming environment for all breeds and species. Our beloved Mac, who passed away in 2025, was the inspiration behind the name.

Meet Desi

Our sweet girl Desi, is the inspiration around making sure even our reactive furry friends have the safe space needed to enjoy life just as much as any other dog, which includes getting primped and pampered.

We take safety and remaining low stress very seriously. Part of the reason our pricing might seem higher than our competitors is because we only book in two dogs from different families at a time.

Safe Space

We’ve designed our space so dogs who do not know each other are kept as far away from each other as possible. Your pup will not be placed in stacked kennels on top of other unknown dogs.

Feline Friends

If you are one of our feline patrons, your cat will be the only animal on the salon floor while they are being groomed. It's all about keepin' it real cool for your kitty!

Top Priority

Safety is a top priority at Mac Daddy Grooming, second only to love. We're all about making your pet feel like the coolest cat in town!
